Your Questions
Answered

Everything you need to know about the RB Fitness ecosystem and elite memberships.

Can I freeze my membership?

expand_more

Yes, memberships can be frozen for up to 3 months per year for medical or travel reasons. Simply contact member services via the app 7 days in advance.

Are classes included in all plans?

expand_more

Classes are included in Black Label and Elite memberships. Core members can purchase class bundles separately at a discounted rate.

Do you offer nutrition plans?

expand_more

Nutrition is a core pillar of our Elite membership. For Core and Black members, custom meal protocols can be added through our on-site sports nutritionist.

What is the cancellation policy?

expand_more

We believe in earned loyalty, not legal traps. You can cancel with a 30-day notice period. No hidden buyout fees or complicated paperwork.
